Verizon Internship

This past summer (2018), I worked at Verizon as a Data Analtics and Artificial Intelligence intern. I worked on two chatbots over the course of my internship, and learned a great deal about the chatbot sphere. I first worked on the Verizon Career Chatbot which is an external facing platform that is to be used by potential Verizon employee applicants. Users can use the chatbot to get answers to questions about basic application questions.

I then integrated a Google Calendar Service with the already existing Verizon Digital Assistant, which is an internal chatbot used by emploeyes. My work allows Verizon employees to schedule meetings with other employees through the chatbot, and all parties' availabilities will be automatically checked at the given time. If a party is unavailable at the given time, the bot will propose alternative times that will work for all parties.

I learned a lot about artificial intelligence and machine learning over the course of my internship, and I enjoyed learning how to use tools such as DialogFlow!
